# kkr-academy
The ultimate resource hub for B.Tech students. Master your curriculum and get industry-ready with curated coding platforms and certification portals.
KKR Academy is a next-generation digital learning platform designed specifically for B.Tech students. It provides a structured and interactive way to explore subjects, understand concepts, and prepare effectively for exams—all in one place.

The platform is organized subject-wise, where each subject contains multiple lessons (minimum 10 per subject). Every lesson is connected to relevant video content that opens directly from YouTube, allowing students to learn visually and clearly.

In addition to lessons, KKR Academy offers quick access to previous year question papers and quizzes, which open through Google search for easy and wide resource availability. This helps students practice and strengthen their understanding of each subject.

A built-in AI Assistant is available for every subject to instantly clear doubts and provide accurate answers, making learning more interactive and personalized.
